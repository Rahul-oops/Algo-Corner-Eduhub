Success Stories

Preparing for a Career in Coding: Journey to Experience

Roddur Majumder

Title: A Tale of Grit, Learning, and Success

Introduction: In the bustling corridors of IIT Kharagpur, where dreams take flight and ambitions soar high, I embarked on a journey that would shape my career and define my aspirations. From the moment I stepped into the hallowed halls of this prestigious institution, I was driven by a passion for coding and a thirst for knowledge that knew no bounds.

Chapter 1: The Genesis - A Spark Ignited: As a wide-eyed freshman, I was introduced to the world of programming and data structures, and it was here that the seeds of curiosity were sown within me. Delving into the intricacies of C/C++, I found myself captivated by the elegant logic and sheer power of coding. With each problem solved on platforms like Codeforces, Codechef, Leetcode, and GeekforGeeks, my hunger for learning only grew stronger.

Chapter 2: Summer Break - A Time for Intensive Learning: With the summer vacation after my fourth semester looming on the horizon, I seized the opportunity to dive deeper into the realm of programming. Armed with determination and fueled by the prospect of upcoming internship drives, I immersed myself in mastering the nuances of data structures and algorithms. I learned some important CS fundamental subjects like : Object oriented programming , Operation systems and database management system etc.

Chapter 3: Amazon - A Summer of Growth and Discovery: The culmination of my efforts materialized in the form of an internship opportunity at Amazon India. Stepping into the dynamic environment of one of the world's leading tech giants, I found myself surrounded by a cohort of talented professionals who would become my mentors and guides. Working primarily with Java, JUnit, Mockito, and a myriad of Amazon-specific development tools, I honed my skills and expanded my horizons with each passing day. The invaluable experience gained during this on-site internship left an indelible mark on my professional journey.

Chapter 4: The Road to Placement - Trials and Triumphs: Armed with newfound knowledge and bolstered by the confidence gained at Amazon, I embarked on the arduous path of placement preparation. With the competitive landscape of my college looming large, I threw myself into the fray with unwavering determination. From solving coding contests on various platforms to undertaking full-stack projects, I left no stone unturned in my pursuit of excellence. Delving into advanced topics such as system design and machine learning, I fortified my arsenal of skills, readying myself for the challenges that lay ahead.

Chapter 5: The Culmination - A Dream Realized : Amidst a sea of rejections and setbacks, I persevered, undeterred in my pursuit of success. And finally, after countless trials and tribulations, my efforts bore fruit as I received an offer from a prestigious company in Bangalore. As I reflect on the journey that brought me to this moment, I am filled with a profound sense of gratitude for the lessons learned, the challenges overcome, and the unwavering support of my peers and mentors.

Conclusion: My journey from IIT Kharagpur to landing a dream internship and job has been a testament to the power of perseverance, dedication, and continuous learning. With each obstacle encountered and each milestone achieved, I have emerged stronger and more resilient than before. As I look towards the future with optimism and excitement, I carry with me the invaluable lessons gleaned from my experiences, confident in my ability to tackle whatever challenges may come my way.

Vedanth S

Title : Preparation to Inspiration

My name is Vedanth S, a pre-final year undergraduate at IIT-ISM. Reflecting on my journey, I recall how my pursuit of knowledge and dedication paved the way for remarkable opportunities and achievements. It all began in my first year at college when I delved into the realms of data structures and algorithms. This foundational knowledge laid the groundwork for my subsequent endeavors.

In my second year, I embarked on a journey of continuous learning and growth. I immersed myself in advanced algorithms and delved into the world of competitive programming. Participating in contests on platforms like Codeforces and Codechef not only sharpened my problem-solving skills but also instilled in me a passion for excellence.

As my proficiency in programming soared, I ventured into the realm of web development towards the end of my second year. The allure of creating dynamic and interactive web applications fueled my curiosity, prompting me to delve deeper into this domain. This newfound expertise led to an enriching on-campus internship opportunity at Honeywell, where I honed my skills as a software developer.

In my third year, armed with newfound knowledge and experience, I embarked on ambitious projects in web development. Applying the principles and techniques I had learned, I crafted innovative solutions and honed my craft further. Concurrently, I delved into the intricacies of computer science fundamentals, broadening my understanding of the underlying principles that govern technology. With a comprehensive skill set and a passion for excellence, I ventured into the competitive realm of campus placements. Drawing upon my learning journey and practical experience, I secured a coveted on-campus placement at Mercari for the role of software developer. This achievement stands as a testament to the dedication, perseverance, and continuous learning that have been the hallmarks of my journey.

Looking back, I am grateful for the opportunities that have come my way and the lessons learned along the path to success. As I embark on the next chapter of my journey, I carry with me the lessons learned and the experiences gained, poised to tackle new challenges and seize new opportunities on the road ahead.
